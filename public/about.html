About Sponsorease

Sponsorease is a platform built to solve the problem of connecting event organizers with the right sponsors. Whether you're hosting a college fest, conference, or startup launch — we help you get funded by verified sponsors who share your mission.

Our system matches your submitted event requests to relevant sponsors based on category, budget, and purpose. Sponsors can browse submissions, express interest, and even schedule meetings directly with organizers.

We're building a bridge between ambition and support — making sponsorships simple, fast, and impactful.
