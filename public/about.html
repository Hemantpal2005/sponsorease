About Sponsorease

Sponsorease is a modern platform designed to simplify the way event organizers and sponsors connect. We eliminate the hassle of finding and securing sponsorships by creating a transparent, efficient, and accessible digital space where opportunities meet support.

Our Mission

To empower events of all sizes — from college fests to corporate expos — by making sponsorship accessible, easy to manage, and effective. We believe in enabling meaningful collaborations between creators and companies.

What We Do

  • Connect event organizers with potential sponsors based on category and interest
  • Offer smart forms to gather detailed event data
  • Allow sponsors to browse events and choose who they want to support
  • Enable in-platform messaging and meeting scheduling
  • Provide a dashboard to track sponsorship progress and interest

Meet the Creators

Hemant Pal is a Computer Science Engineering student at NIT Surat, batch of 2028. With a strong interest in solving real-world problems through technology, Hemant envisioned Sponsorease as a platform to simplify sponsorship connections and empower event creators nationwide.

Sujal Chandwani, co-creator of Sponsorease, is pursuing MSc. Physics at NIT Surat, batch of 2029. With a deep analytical mindset and a passion for impact-driven innovation, Sujal brings strategic insights and technical excellence to the platform.

Together, they created Sponsorease with a shared belief: that organizing events and connecting with the right sponsors shouldn’t be a struggle — it should be simple, direct, and effective.

Message from the Founders

We created Sponsorease to solve a problem we personally experienced — the hassle of knocking on countless doors and sending endless emails to find the right sponsors for great events. With this platform, we’re giving power back to organizers by simplifying the sponsorship process through a single, smart, and scalable solution.

Sponsorease is built to make it easier than ever to connect with sponsors — without needing to walk into 100 different offices. Everything is now online, transparent, and easier to manage.

We hope this platform empowers you to take your events to new heights.

– Hemant & Sujal
